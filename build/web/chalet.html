Chalet House Plans


Looking for a simple getaway home that
astounds the eye and soothes the soul?
Then this is the perfect plan for you!
Be dazzled by an entire wall of windows
that looks out to a glowing sunset on the
lake or behind the mountains. A large
fireplace warms the retreat even in chilly
winter months. The U-shaped kitchen is efficient,
putting everything you need at your fingertips.
Enjoy the outdoor views or a fresh summer breeze
through a sunny window over the kitchen sink.
The spacious master suite is located on the main
floor, making this home ideal for older folks as well.
The master bedroom can be accessed directly or by
going through the laundry room and into the adjoining
master bath. Upstairs, you'll find an enormous second bedroom,
which can also serve as a storage area, office or media room.
The stairway overlooks the vaulted living room, taking advantage
of the gorgeous views the windows provide.
